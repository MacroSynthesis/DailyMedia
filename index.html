7 useful tips and tricks for beginner guitarist

By: Marco Gabriel P. Maniego

Learning the basic chords first

by learning the basic chords of the guitar you can already play countless songs and learning the basic chords is the foundation when it comes to learning the guitar

Posted by Marco on March 9, 2023

Be consistent

Learning guitar requires a lot of time and effort, in order for you to become a better player and see instant results you need to have consistency, you need to practice as much as possible in your free time, back when I was a beginner myself I would constantly practice while watching my favorites shows, after school, even after dying in a game that I was playing, basically what my point here is that practice in every opportunity you have, even when I was outside in my mind I would practice in my imagination which I think really gave me a fast result of learning the guitar.

Posted by Marco on March 9, 2023

Practice one song that has basic chords

Pick a song of your choice that has a basic chord progression such as Huling el bimbo by Eraserheads, Harana by Parokya ni Edgar, etc, and practice learning at least one song at a time for it will improve your timing and you’ll be able to master the changing of chords quickly.

Posted by Marco on March 9, 2023

Learn from your mistakes

Through consistent practice, you’ll be able to determine what works and what doesn’t you can’t just spend endless and countless time mindlessly practicing you also need to practice smart, you need to focus on the basic fundamentals first and to be able to determine your skill level, you need to, crawl before you walk.

Posted by Marco on March 9, 2023

Play with other people

By playing with other musicians you’ll be able to hear constructive feedback and you’ll be able to improve your playing by knowing what you need to work on, It can also spark inspiration by surrounding yourself with great musicians.

Posted by Marco on March 9, 2023

Focus on your technique

You need to learn to hold a pick properly or what works best for you, and focus on your technique when it comes to fretting notes this is one of the basic skills for playing the guitar but a lot of guitar players have formed bad habits when that slows their playing, you need to put your fingers arch your fingers and make sure that they are close to the frets so you can avoid the potential of buzzing

Posted by Marco on March 9, 2023

Practice songs by ear

You’ll be able to play any song you like by using your ear, and you’ll have on-point timing by practicing songs using your ear this is one of the things I wish I could’ve adapted to myself as a beginner guitar player, By learning music by ear, pupils have developed the ability to anticipate song melodies and identify different musical compositional structures.
